ICON PLACEHOLDER

To complete the extension, you need to convert the SVG icons to PNG:

1. icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
2. icon48.png (48x48 pixels)  
3. icon128.png (128x128 pixels)

You can use any tool like:
- Figma
- Adobe Illustrator
- Online SVG to PNG converter
- ImageMagick: `convert icon16.svg icon16.png`

The SVG files in this folder can be used as templates.
